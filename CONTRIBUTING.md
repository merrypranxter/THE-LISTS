# Contributing to The Lists

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the vocabulary entries of The Lists! We appreciate your effort to help expand and refine our collection.

## Guidelines for Adding Vocabulary Entries  
1. **Select Unique Vocabulary:**  
   Ensure that the vocabulary entry you want to add is not already included in the existing list.

2. **Entry Format:**  
   - Each entry should include the word and its definition. 
   - Use proper grammar and punctuation.  
   Example:  
     - **Word:** Example  
     - **Definition:** A representative form or pattern.

3. **Use Reliable Sources:**  
   Reference dictionaries or reputable websites when providing definitions, ensuring accuracy.

## Guidelines for Modifying Existing Entries  
1. **Review Before Editing:**  
   Check the current entry for accuracy and clarity before making edits.

2. **Suggest Improvements:**  
   If you believe there’s a better definition or context, provide a clear justification for your suggestion.

3. **Follow Existing Structure:**  
   Maintain consistency in the format as outlined above to ensure seamless integration into the list.

## Submission Process  
Once you have your changes ready, please follow these steps to submit:
1. Create a new branch for your changes.  
2. Make your additions or modifications.  
3. Submit a pull request describing the changes and the reasoning behind them.

Thank you for your contributions! Happy coding!

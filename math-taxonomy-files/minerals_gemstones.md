## Optical Effects Glossary

### Phenomena

| Effect | Mechanism | Example Minerals |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| **Asterism** | Rutile inclusions reflecting light | Star Ruby, Star Sapphire |
| **Chatoyancy** | Parallel fiber inclusions | Cat's Eye Chrysoberyl, Tiger's Eye |
| **Iridescence** | Thin-film interference | Labradorite, Moonstone |
| **Adularescence** | Light scattering in layers | Moonstone |
| **Play of Color** | Diffraction from silica spheres | Opal |
| **Color Change** | Different absorption in light sources | Alexandrite |
| **Fluorescence** | UV light emission | Fluorite, Scheelite |
| **Phosphorescence** | Afterglow | Some calcites |
| **Tenebrescence** | Reversible color change by light | Hackmanite |
